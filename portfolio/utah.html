Utah Tourist Map

A simple reference map highlighting the five different national parks of Utah.

This reference map was created to promote tourism in the state of Utah. One of Utah’s defining features is its natural beauty, which is best exemplified by its five national parks. Also included are major cities, counties, interstates, lakes, and rivers. This map was entirely created using ArcGIS Pro.
