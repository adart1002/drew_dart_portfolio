Physical Map of Africa

A physical map that details the geography of Africa.

This reference map details the physical geography of Africa. The map includes the countries that constitute the continent, along with some of the longest rivers, largest lakes, highest & lowest elevations, and major cities and capitals. This map was created using ArcGIS Pro with additional improvements and changes made in Adobe Illustrator.
